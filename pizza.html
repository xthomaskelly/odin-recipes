Pizza

Description

This pizza is one of the easiest recipes to make. The taste is way better than you'd expect with a handful of ingredients that are already in your kitchen.

ingredients

  • dough
  • cheese
  • sauce
  • pepperoni

steps

  1. roll out dough
  2. add sauce
  3. add cheese
  4. add pepperoni
  5. cook for a while
  6. let cool, then enjoy
